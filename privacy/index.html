Privacy Policy

At HIGHKEY Games LLC, we take great care in respecting your privacy with regards to any information we may collect while operating our games. The following explains how we collect, use, communicate, disclose and otherwise make use of personal information:

  • We will collect personal information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.
  • We will identify the purposes for which information is being collected before or at the time of collecting personal information.
  • We will collect and use personal information solely for fulfilling those purposes and for other ancillary purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law.
  • We will protect personal information by using reasonable security safeguards against unauthorized access.
  • We will only retain personal information for as long it remains necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes.

We collect gameplay, technical, and behavioral data to better understand our users so we may serve them better. We use Firebase Analytics to do so, and you may find their privacy policy here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/

We also partner with IronSource as a means to serve ads within our game. You may find their privacy policy here: https://developers.ironsrc.com/ironsource-mobile/air/ironsource-mobile-privacy-policy/

HIGHKEY Games LLC may change this privacy policy from time to time at its sole discretion.
